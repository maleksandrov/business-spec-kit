## Core Terms

| Term | Definition | Usage Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|
| ICP | Ideal Customer Profile | Spell out on first use in any document |
| GTM | Go-to-Market | Use "go-to-market" (lowercase, hyphenated) in external documents |
| BD | Business Development | Internal use only; use "partnerships" in external documents |
| POC | Proof of Concept | Spell out on first use; do not use "POC" in external proposals |
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue | Always pair with a number when used; never use vaguely |

## Terms to Avoid

<!--
  Words or phrases that should never appear in outputs — brand-wrong, legally risky, or imprecise.
-->

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|------------|
| "best-in-class" | Specific differentiator claim with evidence |
| "synergy" | Specific shared value or outcome |
| "leverage" (as a verb) | "use", "apply", or "build on" |
| "game-changing" | Specific impact claim |
| "solution" (generic) | Name the actual product or service |
